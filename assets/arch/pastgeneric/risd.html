some type specimens

I find that it is usually nice to be able to put a face to a name. "Oh, there goes Alice" i might think, or "Hello, Thomas!" But i wondered also what that might mean if i could do it with typefaces.

Why shouldn't typefaces also be things we recognize, that have a life of their own outside of ours? This blog is full of specimens which hope to give a face and a name to type we often see around us.
