Projects

Here are some cool projects that i have been exposed to or that i enjoy using.

  • Piggy Bank is a tool for scraping semantic data from webpages
  • Chickenfoot is the best tool for automating web actions, but it is also great for prototyping javascript on your webpages
  • A Day In The Life of MIT students. The project is managed, coded and designed by Reid, whose link lives above.
  • Unison is a file synchronizer that i use to keep religious backups of my data. I have also compiled a unison 2.9.1 ppc binary. It's for anyone running MacOS X at MIT that would like to synchronize with MIT's version of unison.
