What's a triangle?

quick! quick!

There are triangles everywhere. Quick! Pick three things you like, you've got a triangle! Pick a line and a point, it's a triangle! Find three freckles on someone's face and you've got another one. There are triangles everywhere.

I was walking around my apartment when I looked at the door and saw our temporary parking permit dangling on the door, suspended by a magnet. Inside the large four, I saw clear as day a triangle. Granted, the four had a triangular shape, but so did the inside.

The three lines of the number four frame a triangle with a kind of intentional precision. Imagine taking a spoon and a knife and fork and making a triangle out of them. The effect is the same, except with the four the side-effect is the appearance of a number instead of three utensils. Quick, look at that seven over there. I think it's hiding something...
