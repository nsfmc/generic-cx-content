Reuse is a monospace typeface designed for folding letters from pieces of paper. 

In its lowercase form, it resembles the folded letters, while in the uppercase form, it provides a series of dotted and solid lines to indicate folds and tears.

*[download reuse here](http://dl.dropbox.com/u/406291/fonts/reuse.otf)*

Reuse is a postscript flavored opentype font. It is licensed under an MIT-style license which basically means you can do whatever you'd like to it. UFOs or VFDs available on request.
